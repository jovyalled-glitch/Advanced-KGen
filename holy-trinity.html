Holy Trinity

The Holy Trinity refers to the Christian belief in one God in three Persons: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit.

Each Person of the Trinity is distinct, yet they share the same essence, working in unity throughout creation, redemption, and guidance.

The Three Persons

  • God the Father — Creator of all things.
  • God the Son — Jesus Christ, saviour and Redeemer.
  • God the Holy Spirit — The helper, counsellor, and guide.

Key Belief

Christians believe the Trinity shows God’s love and unity, expressed in different ways but always connected as one.
